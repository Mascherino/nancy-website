Introducing Nancy
Nancy is a Discord (and Telegram and others, more on that later) notification bot for the web3 farming game Sunflower Land. Originally created in 2023, it now watches over more than 600 farms.

All notifications can be enabled and disabled using the /setalerts command. In addition to enabling and disabling you can also set a custom offset for alerts. The offset (positive or negative) is applied to all automated notifications. For example you can set the offset for `Everything` to -10, so you get your notifications 10 seconds earlier, giving you time to open the game and be ready for harvesting. For more information, look for the /offset command under Commands

Getting started
While receiving notifications from Nancy is possible on multiple platforms, changing your settings, adding your farm and using the commands is only possible on Discord. To get started join the Discord server with this link: Nancy's Island Once you have joined, you should open a direct message with Nancy, and add your farm with the /add_farm command. This will add your farm to the database, and schedule it to be updated during the next update cycle. After adding your farm, it can take up to 15 minutes for your farm to updated.
